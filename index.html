BearMUN 2019

October 5th & 6th, 2019


Letter from the Secretary-General

Hello!

My name is Tanner Yamada, and I could not be prouder to be the Secretary-General for the very first BearMUN!

After two years of planning and countless hours of blood, sweat, and tears poured into the creation of this conference, we feel confident that delegates will be challenged and pushed in the fiercely creative committees our staff has tirelessly brought to life. This year, our committees range from the North Korean Board of Longevity to the Republican National Committee 2015 to the Security Crisis in Venezuela.

Our annual collegiate conference, held in San Francisco, takes place just a few miles away from where the United Nations charter was officially signed in 1945. We have carried the ideals upon which the UN was founded through over twenty-three years of our collegiate conference, and we now hope to extend our experiences to the high school circuit. Berkeley has long been home to the ideals of free speech and thought, and cultivated a culture that encourages delegates to challenge the status quo. Our committee topics this year reflect those principles in conjunction with those of the fast-paced, technologically driven culture of Silicon Valley. We invite you to join us this year from October 5th to October 6th, 2019, for this exciting experience!
