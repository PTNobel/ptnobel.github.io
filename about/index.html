About

UC Berkeleyâ€™s competitive Model United Nations team (UCBMUN) is proud to announce our inaugural high school conference, BearMUN, for Fall 2019!

BearMUN will be held on the UC Berkeley campus in October of 2019 and will primarily consist of crisis committees, as well as a few larger, directive-based committees. BearMUN committees will be staffed, chaired, and guided by nationally-ranked collegiate delegates competing at the top of their circuit.

We are modeling BearMUN on our pre-existing college conference to ensure that delegates receive a competitive and fun experience with the quality of a collegiate-level event. Delegates attending BearMUN will have many opportunities to interact with individuals who have significant experience competing and staffing at the collegiate level. BearMUN is separate from the springtime BMUN conference, which is hosted by a different, non-competitive club at UC Berkeley.

We want to ensure that attendees enjoy our conference outside of committee as well. Delegates interested in learning more about UC Berkeley (or college life in general) will have the opportunity to attend a BearMUN-organized campus tour, hike, and panel about student life, college applications, internships/research opportunities, and much more.

We are excited to see you all for a weekend of fun and MUN at Berkeley this fall!
